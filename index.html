Our Approach

Three ways we deploy capital

→

Direct Investments

We invest €2–10M in Dutch tech scale-ups with proven product-market fit. Hands-on, as co-entrepreneurs. We build alongside founders for the long term.

◎

Fund-to-Fund

Through select funds like Insiders Ventures, Sky9 Capital, and Slingshot, we build exposure to pre-seed and seed AI technology worldwide. From Silicon Valley to Singapore.

⚡

SPV Follow-on

When a portfolio company breaks out, we double down through dedicated SPVs. Fast, decisive, without fund-structure bureaucracy. This is how we built positions in Anthropic and Revolut.
