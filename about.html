About Shoe Investments

Built on a legacy of entrepreneurship

01

Heritage

Shoe Investments is the family office of Pieter Schoen, an entrepreneur whose family legacy dates back to 1722. After building NLE into the 4th largest energy company in the Netherlands, Pieter turned his focus to investing in the next generation of exceptional founders and fund managers.

02

Strategy

We deploy capital through three distinct channels: direct investments in Dutch tech scale-ups (€2–10M), fund-to-fund positions in select global funds, and SPV follow-on investments in breakout companies like Anthropic and Revolut.

03

Evergreen Advantage

As an evergreen family office, we have no fund cycles, no exit pressure, and no LP approval processes. We commit in days, not quarters. We think in decades, not fund terms. This is our competitive edge.

Let's build something together

Whether you're a fund manager looking for a decisive LP or a founder seeking a hands-on partner — we'd love to hear from you.
